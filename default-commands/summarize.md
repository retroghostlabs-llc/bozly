# Summarize

Create a structured summary of the provided content:
1. **Key Points**: 3-5 most important ideas
2. **Action Items**: Any next steps or todos
3. **Context**: Who should care? Why is it important?
4. **Questions**: What's unclear or needs more depth?

Format as a concise, scannable summary suitable for busy professionals.
